What do I get with the $29 data packs?

Our $29 data packs are ready-to-use, pre-scraped datasets across all our verticals: B2B lead databases (company names, emails, phone numbers, job titles), historic hotel pricing data, rental market snapshots, and e-commerce product/pricing datasets. How do you handle anti-bot protections?

We use a combination of residential proxies, browser fingerprint rotation, headless browser automation, request throttling, and CAPTCHA solving services. Our infrastructure is constantly updated to handle the latest anti-bot measures from major websites.

Is web scraping legal?

Web scraping of publicly available data is generally legal. We scrape only public-facing websites and comply with applicable data protection regulations. We advise clients on best practices and ensure our methods respect robots.txt and terms of service where required.

How fast can I get my data?

Ready-to-use data packs (leads, hotel prices, rental data, e-commerce snapshots) are available for instant download.
